Steak

This Steak is a delicious staple! Seer it up and you will not be disappointed.

  • 1 pond steak
  • Gralic
  • Oil
  • Chives
  • Rosemary
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Turn on grill
  2. Let it heat up to 400 degrees
  3. Season Steak
  4. Add steak to the grill
  5. Cook on both sides for 3 minutes
