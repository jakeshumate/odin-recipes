Pesto Pasta

This pesto dish has been passed down through generations in my family. It is made with our famous pesto.

Ingredients

  • Basil
  • Cheese
  • Oil
  • Nuts
  • Garlic
  • Lemon Juice
  • Pasta

Steps

  1. Mix basil and cheese
  2. add to a food processor
  3. mix for a few seconds
  4. add in nuts, garlic and lemon juice
  5. Mix for 30 seconds
  6. Add pasta to boiling water
